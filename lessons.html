Group Lessons for Beginners

If you and your companion have never tried kitesurfing before, then the group lessons for beginners are exactly made for you. The two of you will be a team with your personal certified instructor. Our groups are never more than two people to make sure you have a successful course. How quickly someone will learn to handle the kite or stand up on the board varies from person to person. Nevertheless, our experienced instructors try their best to bring you as far as possible.

  • 2 days 3 hours kitesurfing lessons
  • kitesurfing gear included
  • intro to the weather & spot
  • theory & safety procedures
  • kite set up and control exercises
  • body grag in the water
  • kite re-launce from the water
  • water start into both direction
